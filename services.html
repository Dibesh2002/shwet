How We Can Help You

At Shwet Bhairab Architect and Association, we offer a comprehensive range of architectural services to meet diverse client needs. From initial concept to final construction, our team provides expert guidance and innovative solutions at every stage of your project.

Each service is delivered with our commitment to design excellence, technical precision, and client satisfaction. We tailor our approach to your specific requirements, ensuring results that exceed expectations.

Our Approach to Pricing

We understand that every project is unique, with its own scope, complexity, and requirements. That's why we offer flexible fee structures tailored to your specific project needs:

  • Fixed Fee: A predetermined amount based on the project scope and deliverables.
  • Percentage of Construction Cost: Fee calculated as a percentage of the total construction budget.
  • Hourly Rate: Charges based on the actual time spent on your project.
  • Hybrid Approach: A combination of the above methods to best suit your project.

We provide transparent fee proposals that clearly outline our services and associated costs. Contact us to discuss your project and receive a customized fee proposal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the architectural design process take?

The timeline varies depending on project size and complexity. A typical residential project might take 3-6 months for design and documentation, while larger commercial projects can take 6-12 months or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you handle permits and approvals?

Yes, we assist with preparing and submitting documentation for building permits and other regulatory approvals. We have extensive experience navigating local building codes and zoning requirements.

Can you work with my existing contractor?

Absolutely. We're happy to collaborate with your chosen contractor. We can also recommend qualified contractors from our network if needed.

What information do you need to provide an estimate?

To provide an accurate estimate, we typically need details about the project scope, size, location, budget, timeline, and any specific requirements or constraints. The more information you can provide, the more precise our estimate will be.

Do you offer sustainable/green design services?

Yes, sustainability is integral to our design philosophy. We offer comprehensive sustainable design services, including energy-efficient design, passive strategies, renewable energy integration, and assistance with green building certifications.
